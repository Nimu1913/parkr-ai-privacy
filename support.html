How do I analyze a parking sign?

Simply tap the camera icon or upload photo button on the home screen. Take a clear photo of the parking sign, and our AI will analyze it within seconds to explain the parking rules in plain language.

Is my personal data safe?

Absolutely! Parkr AI is designed with privacy-first principles:

  • All image processing happens locally on your device
  • License plates and faces are automatically blurred before any upload
  • We don't store your photos
  • No personal information is tracked or sold

What countries and cities are supported?

Parkr AI works with parking signs worldwide! We have special optimization for:

  • United States (all major cities)
  • Canada
  • United Kingdom
  • Sweden, Norway, Denmark, Finland (Nordic countries)
  • Germany, France, Netherlands (EU countries)
  • Australia

Even if your city isn't listed, our AI can interpret parking signs in most languages and formats.

How accurate is the parking sign analysis?

Our AI-powered analysis is highly accurate, utilizing advanced vision recognition and natural language processing. However, always verify with the actual sign and local regulations. Parkr AI is a helpful tool but should not replace your judgment.

The app isn't analyzing my photo. What should I do?

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Check that camera permissions are enabled in Settings
  • Take a clearer, well-lit photo of the sign
  • Restart the app
  • Update to the latest version

Does Parkr AI work offline?

The AI analysis requires an internet connection. However, previously analyzed signs are cached locally for quick reference.

How do I manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID:

  1. Open iOS Settings
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Select "Subscriptions"
  4. Find "Parkr AI" and manage or cancel
